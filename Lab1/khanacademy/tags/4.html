Pasta Alfredo

Ingredient Amount
Pasta 200 g
Cream 200 ml
Butter 30 g
Cheese 50 g
  • Italian dish
  • Cooking time: 20 minutes

Boil the pasta. Melt butter in a pan, add cream and heat it. Add cheese and mix. Combine sauce with pasta.

Plov

Ingredient Amount
Rice 2 cups
Meat 400 g
Carrots 2
Onion 1
  • Traditional Central Asian food
  • Very filling

Fry meat and onions. Add carrots and cook. Add rice and water. Cook on low heat until ready.

Beshbarmak

Ingredient Amount
Meat 1 kg
Dough 500 g
Onion 2
  • Kazakh national dish
  • Served hot

Boil meat until soft. Cut dough into pieces and cook it. Serve meat and dough together with onions.
